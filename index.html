01 — Our Philosophy

Pain is not a problem.
It's a message.

Most people with chronic pain don't have a broken body. They have a nervous system that learned to protect itself — and never received the information that it's safe to move again.

Our work is to deliver the right inputs: safety, control, capacity. Your body isn't failing. It's waiting for the right information.

"Pain is your nervous system's best guess about threat — not a verdict about your body."

I · Pain

A strategy, not a sentence

Pain appears when your nervous system interprets your body as being at risk. We change the interpretation — giving it evidence that movement is safe.

II · Health

Capacity to carry load without fear

Health isn't the absence of symptoms. It's the presence of confidence, strength, and freedom of movement. It's being able to trust your own body.

III · Your outcome

Adaptive capacity — not sessions

We don't sell appointments. We rebuild the version of you that runs without calculating risk, plays with your kids without thinking, lives without negotiating with your body.

06 — Rolfing® in depth

A conversation with
your body's architecture.

Developed by Dr. Ida Rolf, Rolfing® Structural Integration works with the fascia — the three-dimensional web of connective tissue that surrounds every muscle and joint. When fascia becomes dense or misaligned, it creates chronic tension, postural compensations, and pain that other therapies struggle to resolve.

Rolfing® combines deep manual therapy with movement reeducation. The manual component releases restrictions; the movement component teaches new patterns of alignment and ease. Together, they don't just treat symptoms — they reorganize how you inhabit your body.

Manual Therapy

Deep fascial work

Precise, deep manipulation that releases tension and restrictions in the myofascial network. Produces immediate increases in range of motion and noticeable pain relief from the very first session.

Movement Reeducation

New patterns, new possibilities

The dynamic side of the work. Focused on posture, body awareness, and optimizing functional movement. Prevents relapses and ensures long-term results that extend far beyond the session.

Chronic pain relief

Addresses the root causes of persistent pain by resolving the fascial tensions and postural patterns that create it.

Greater range of motion

Restrictions release. Joints move more freely. Your body rediscovers movements it had forgotten were possible.

Improved posture

Fascial reorganization leads to natural postural alignment — not forced correction. You stand, walk, and breathe differently.

Body awareness

You start to feel the subtle patterns you didn't notice before — and gain the tools to change them consciously.

Tension release

Muscle fatigue, tightness, and holding patterns that accumulate over years begin to dissolve — often permanently.

Better sleep quality

With the nervous system less activated and the body more at ease, sleep deepens. Recovery happens at a different level.

08 — Before you book

Common
questions.

If you can't find what you're looking for here, send us a message — we're happy to answer directly.

Do you offer a free consultation?

Yes. Every new client starts with a free 15-minute consultation call — whether by phone or video. It's a short conversation to understand your situation, answer your questions, and direct you to the best path forward. No commitment required.

What's the difference between Rolfing® and massage or physiotherapy?

Rolfing® works specifically with the fascial network — the connective tissue that surrounds every structure in your body. Unlike massage (which mainly addresses muscle tension) or traditional physiotherapy (which targets specific joints or conditions), Rolfing® reorganizes the body as an integrated whole. It combines deep manual therapy with movement reeducation, which is why results tend to be longer-lasting.

How many sessions will I need?

It depends on your goals. For Rolfing®, the classical Ten-Series is a complete structural reorganization. Many people also benefit from shorter non-standardized programs (3-5 sessions) focused on specific complaints. Training programs are typically structured in 8-12 week cycles. We'll discuss options during your free consultation.

Do you offer direct billing or accept insurance?

Rolfing® sessions through Active Care Physiotherapy are covered by most extended health plans in Ontario — typically under registered physiotherapy or massage therapy benefits. We provide receipts for reimbursement. Ask during your free consultation and we'll clarify what applies to your specific plan.

Do I need to be an athlete to train with Jeymison?

Not at all. Most of our clients aren't competitive athletes. They're people who want to move well, feel strong, and live without chronic pain — whether that means returning to a sport, getting back into training after a pause, or simply being able to do daily activities without their body complaining. Every program is built around where you are, not where an athlete would be.

Can I do both Rolfing® and Training?

Absolutely — and for many people, that's the most powerful combination. Rolfing® reorganizes structure; Training builds capacity on top of that new structure. The two practices work in dialogue, and the results compound. We'll help you sequence them correctly based on your priorities.

Do you work with people outside London, Ontario?

Yes — online training with Jeymison is available anywhere. We also welcome visitors to London, ON for intensive Rolfing® sessions if you're willing to travel. Reach out and we'll find the format that works.
